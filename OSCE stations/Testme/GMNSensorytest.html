Gross Motor Neurone Sensory Examination Test

Questions

1. Soft Touch

2. Sharp Touch

3. Vibration

4.Proprioception

5. Reflexes

Answers: hover over to reveal

Soft Touch

Use cotton wool
Acknowledge there are different dermatomes
Touch different parts of the limb - down one side, across fingers/toes and up the inside
Compare both sides

Sharp Touch

Use the sharp red tool
Acknowledge there are different dermatomes
Touch different parts of the limb - down one side, across fingers/toes and up the inside
Compare both sides

Vibration

Use tuning fork on:
Interphalangeal (joint on thumb)
Bony part of big toe

Proprioception

Ask patient to close their Eyes
Change position of thumb/big toe and ask them if it is up or down

Reflexes

Use tendon hammer
Upper Limb: Wrist, Inner elbow and outer elbow
Lower Limb: Knee, ankle, clonus and feet planters
Scoring 0-4+ (0 = no response 2= normal 4=hyper-responsive)
