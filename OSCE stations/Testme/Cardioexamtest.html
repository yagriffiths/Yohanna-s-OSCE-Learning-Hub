Ausculation

Listen to all 4 valves:
Aortic - 2nd Intercostal Space Right
Pulmonary - 2nd Intercostal Space Left
Tricuspid - 5th Intercostal Space - lower left
Mitral - 5th intercostal space - Midclavicular line
Listen to both lungs

S1: First heart sound and the start of ventricular systole
S2: Second heart sound marks the end of ventricular systole and the start of diastole
S3: Heart murmer that occurs during early diastole, immediately after S2
S4: Heart Mumer that occurs just before S1 at the end of diastole
