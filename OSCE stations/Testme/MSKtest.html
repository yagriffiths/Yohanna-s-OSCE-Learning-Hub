What is extension and flexion?

Flexion decreases the angle between the bones (bending of the joint)
Extension: straightening from a flexed position or bending backward

What is Abduction and Adduction?

Abduction: Moving the limb or hand laterally away from the body, or spreading the fingers or toes.
Adduction: brings the limb or hand toward or across the midline of the body, or brings the fingers or toes together.
