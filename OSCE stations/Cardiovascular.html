Hands

Pulses

  • Brachial
  • Carotid

Neck

Check for JVP and measure using 2 rulers

  1. identify landmarks: Sternocleidomastoid, sternoclavicular, External jugular vein, angle of louis
  2. Identify the heighest point
  3. Explain how to initiate Hepatojugular reflex

  3. Thrills

Auscultation

Listen to all 4 valves

  1. Aortic - 2nd Intercostal Space Right
  2. Pulmonary - 2nd Intercostal Space Left
  3. Tricuspid - 5th Intercostal Space - lower left
  4. Mitral - 5th intercostal space - Midclavicular line
  5. Listen to both lungs

Explain the following sounds

  1. S1 and S2
  2. S3 - what does it signify?
  3. S4 - what does it signify?
