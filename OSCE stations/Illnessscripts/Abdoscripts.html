Adbdominal Illness Scripts

1. Acute Mesenteric Ischemia

Symptoms

  • Nausea, vomiting and anorexia
  • Severe generalised abdominal pain
  • Diarrhoea progressing to obstipation
  • necrosis can occur from 6 hours of onset leading to symptoms of shock

2. Acute bowel obstruction

Symptoms

  • abdominal distention
  • nausea and vomiting
  • cramping & pain
  • ribbon like stools

3. Acute appendicitis

Symptoms

  • Pain
  • Nausea, anorexia, vomiting following the onset of pain
  • fever
  • Diarrhoea
  • malaise
  • hurts to cough

3. Acute pancreatitis

Symptoms

  • Epigastric pain (severe) radiating into the back which may improve on leaning forward
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Fever & tachy
  • sometimes diarrhoea
  • Jaundice (28%)

4. Ectopic pregnancy

Symptoms

  • Lower abdominal pain on one side
  • Amenorrhoea
  • PV loss
  • Referred shoulder pain
  • Continuous urge to defecate
  • symptoms of shock if ruptured
