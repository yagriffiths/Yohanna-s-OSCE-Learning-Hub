Respiritory Illness Scripts

1. Asthma

Symptoms

  • Wheeze
  • Cough
  • Breathlessness
  • Chest tightness

2. Pleural Effusion

Symptoms

  • Breathlessness +/- hypoxia
  • Reduced expansion
  • Reduced or absent breath sounds
  • Stoney dull to percuss
  • Can be bilateral or unilateral

3. Pneumonia

Symptoms

  • Cough (productive)
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fever
  • Hypoxia / cyanosis
  • Breathlessness
  • Crackles
  • Dull to percuss

4. Pneumothorax

Symptoms

  • Sudden onset breathlessness
  • pleuritic chest pain
  • More common in males
  • More common in Asthma & COPD
