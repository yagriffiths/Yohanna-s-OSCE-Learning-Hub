CVS illness Scripts

1. Aortic dissection

Symptoms

  • Abrupt, severe chest/back/abdominal pain that is described as ripping/tearing
  • Pain may migrate as the dissection extends
  • Perfusion deficit; Left and right BP differential, pulse deficit
  • Minority may present with syncope without pain and/or signs of limb ischaemia
  • diastolic murmur
  • Shock
>

2. Aortic stenosis

Symptoms

  • Fatigue
  • Dyspnoea on exertion
  • Orthopnoea
  • Exertional chest pain
  • Exertional syncope
  • Systolic murmur
  • Systolic thrill at the end of expiration

3. Pulmonary embolism

Symptoms

  • Abrupt onset of pleuritic chest pain
  • SOB/tachypnea -exertional
  • tachycardia
  • fever
  • cough/heamoptysis
  • may be non specific symptoms and the “classic” presentation is often not seen

4. Acute Pericarditis

Symptoms

  • Sharp pleuritic chest pain
  • low grade fever
  • dyspnea/tachypnea
  • cough
